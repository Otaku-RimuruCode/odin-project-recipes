Loaded Califlower

A great way to add some zip to your cauliflower.
This recipe is low-carb and resembles a loaded potato!
Very unique and down right tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dry ranch salad dressing mix (such as Hidden Valley Ranch®)
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter, cut into small pieces, or more to taste

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
    Bring water to a boil. Add cauliflower, cover, and steam until very tender,
    15 to 20 minutes. Transfer cauliflower to a bowl, mash, and strain excess water.

  3. Step 3

    Mix cauliflower, sour cream, Cheddar cheese, ranch dressing mix, onion powder,
    and garlic powder together in a 9-inch baking dish; top with butter.

  4. Step 4

    Bake in the preheated oven until bubbling, 30 to 45 minutes.
